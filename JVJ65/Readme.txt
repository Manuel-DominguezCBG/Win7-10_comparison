Contents of this folder:

1) Data_input: A folder to put the samples and create one csv file per run.
2) Data_output: Where the output files are. These are the ones that are analysed.
3) JVJ65_Merge_all_needed_samples_data_in_one_CSV_file. A notebook to merge all samples in one csv files.
4) Win7 and Win10 are the samples (the raw data).
5) JVJ65_Analysis. A notebook to do the analysis.
